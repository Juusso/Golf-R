MK7.5 Golf R Mechanical Notes

Suspension Tuning

Roll center correction kit from whiteline shows significant re-alignment to stock control arm geometry when pait with aftermarket suspension or springs.

Additonaly, roll center kit allows for adjustment of camber up to -2.5 degrees for either a more aggressive offset or to further correction alignment back to stock. Locking nuts for the roll centre kit, however, Interrupt test emphasize italic are shown to loosen over time. Can look into loctite or small tac weld.

Diagnosing Suspension Issues

Known suspension issues are as follows...

  • Chirping front upper suspension noise most prominent over small bumps at speed
  • Squeeking noise at low speeds most prominenet over speed bumps
  • Rear knocking at low speeds most prominent over speed bumps
  • Clunking front lower suspension most prominent with deflection on LCAS
